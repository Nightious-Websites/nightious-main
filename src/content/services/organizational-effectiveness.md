## What's included

- **Tool audit** — Identify redundant subscriptions and gaps in your current software stack.
- **Project management setup** — Notion, ClickUp, Asana, or Linear configured for how your team actually works.
- **Workflow design** — Document and optimize your core business processes from intake to delivery.
- **Automation mapping** — Identify repetitive tasks and build automations (Zapier, Make, n8n) to eliminate them.
- **Team onboarding systems** — SOPs, wikis, and runbooks so new hires get up to speed without hand-holding.
- **Meeting and communication structure** — Async-first communication frameworks that reduce meeting overhead.

## Why Nightious for Organizational Effectiveness

- We don't impose cookie-cutter frameworks — your operations reflect how your team actually thinks.
- ROI-first: every recommendation is evaluated against the time and cost it saves.
- We stay involved through implementation, not just strategy delivery.

## What's included

- **Privacy audit** — Review of your current tools, accounts, and data exposure across the web.
- **VPN setup** — Selection and configuration of a VPN appropriate for your threat model (business, travel, remote work).
- **Password manager migration** — Move to 1Password, Bitwarden, or similar with full team onboarding.
- **Two-factor authentication** — Enable and configure 2FA across all critical accounts.
- **Data breach monitoring** — Set up alerts for when your email or credentials appear in known breaches.
- **Device hardening** — Browser settings, DNS over HTTPS, and telemetry reduction for Windows and macOS.
- **Secure communication** — Signal, ProtonMail, or similar recommendations based on your needs.

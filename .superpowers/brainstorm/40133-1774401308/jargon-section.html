How should the Jargon → Plain English section feel?

This is Nightious's core brand moment — the hero is now minimal, so this section carries the "we're different" message. It needs to be unforgettable.

A — Morph & Glitch

One jargon phrase at a time, large and centered. GSAP animates the jargon text "corrupting" (glitch effect), then morphs into the plain English equivalent. Cycles through 4 pairs. Scanline overlay reinforces the futuristic feel. Scroll-triggered and looping.

B — Dual Terminal

Two glass terminal panels side by side — left scrolls jargon (red-tinted, faded), right outputs the plain English (purple, glowing). Lines appear in sync as you scroll. Familiar from the existing PainStatement but elevated to feel like a live translation console.

Full-width comparison rows that stagger in as you scroll. Left column = jargon (muted, red-tinted), right = plain English (glowing purple). Header labels anchor the comparison. Clean, readable, and it scales easily to show 4–6 pairs without feeling cluttered.

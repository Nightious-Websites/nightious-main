Hero H1 Options

Each pair shows: white line + gradient line (purple→pink), as it'll appear on the dark particle background. Research says: shorter, bolder, scannable. All under 8 words total.

YOUR TECH, SORTED.
YOUR BUSINESS, PROTECTED.

A — "Sorted / Protected"

Short and punchy. Each line is a promise. "Sorted" = tech handled. "Protected" = business safe. Inspired by the motto — quiet confidence without spelling it out.

WE HANDLE THE TECH.
YOU RUN THE SHOW.

B — "We / You"

Clear division of responsibility. We take the burden, you stay in control. Direct, practical, empowering. Good for small business owners who just want things handled.

TECHNOLOGY MADE SIMPLE.
SUPPORT MADE PERSONAL.

C — "Simple / Personal"

Two core brand pillars in one line each. Speaks to both the product (simplified tech) and the relationship (personal service). Slightly softer and more approachable.

YOUR DIGITAL TEAM,
READY WHEN YOU ARE.

D — "Your Team / Ready"

Closest to the motto's spirit — "your team" creates ownership and belonging, "ready when you are" signals availability and dedication. Warm and partnership-focused.

LESS TECH STRESS.
MORE BUSINESS GROWTH.

E — "Less / More"

Pain → gain in two lines. Speaks directly to the small business owner's frustration and desire. Very scan-friendly — each line is a clear before/after. Highest conversion potential based on the research.
